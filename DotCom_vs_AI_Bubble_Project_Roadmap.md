# Dot-com Bubble vs AI Bubble Analysis

## Project Overview

A reproducible financial research project comparing the Dot-com Bubble
(1995--2002) and the AI-driven market rally (2022--Present). The project
emphasizes data engineering practices, quantitative analysis, and clear
documentation.
